Places to visit

Kinshasa National Museum

Boulevard Triomphal, Kinshasa.

Explore the rich cultural heritage of the Democratic Republic of Congo at this iconic museum.

Marché de la Liberté

456 Market Street, Kinshasa.

A bustling market offering a variety of local goods, crafts, and fresh produce.

Lola Ya Bonobo Sanctuary

Petites Chutes des Lukaya, Kinshasa.

A sanctuary dedicated to the protection and rehabilitation of bonobos, a rare primate species.

Kinshasa Botanical Garden

Avenue Kasa-Vubu, Kinshasa.

A serene escape featuring a diverse collection of plants and flowers native to the region.

Stade des Martyrs

Boulevard Triomphal, Kinshasa.

One of the largest stadiums in Africa, hosting major sports events and concerts.

Kingakati valley

303 Zoo Road, Kinshasa

Home to a variety of wildlife species, offering an educational experience for visitors.

N'Sele River

Nsele , Kinshasa

A picturesque river perfect for boat rides and enjoying the natural beauty of Kinshasa.

Kinshasa Cultural Center

Aviateurs 66, Gombe, Kinshasa

A hub for art, music, and cultural events showcasing the vibrant spirit of Kinshasa.
